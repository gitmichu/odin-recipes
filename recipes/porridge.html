Porridge for breakfast


Description


'Yummy, mummy!' is something you're bound to hear when you make this delicious
breakfast treat. A simple oatmeal porridge with mashed bananas and melted
chocolate make it extra special! You can add raisins and 1/4 teaspoon of cinnamon, depending on what your family likes.


Ingredients


  • 2 cups boiling water
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 banana, mashed
  • ¼ cup semisweet chocolate chips

Steps


  1. In a saucepan, combine water, oats and salt.
  2. Simmer 5 minutes uncovered, stirring occasionally.
  3. Remove from heat, cover, and let stand 3 minutes.
  4. Stir in brown sugar, banana and chocolate chips.
